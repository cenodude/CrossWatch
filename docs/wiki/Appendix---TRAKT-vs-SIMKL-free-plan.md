# Trakt vs SIMKL (Free plans)

> Trakt Free caps hard; SIMKL Free is far looser (watchlist = uncapped).

| Limit                     | TRAKT — Free                          | SIMKL — Free                                                           |
|---------------------------|---------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Watchlist                 | 100 items max                         | No limit (default watchlists)                                          |
| Collection                | 100 items max                         | N/A (use custom lists)                                                 |
| Personal / Custom lists   | 2 lists, 100 items each               | 30 lists, 1,000 items each (up to 60,000 items total across custom)    |
| CSV exports               | VIP only                              | PRO/VIP only                                                           |
